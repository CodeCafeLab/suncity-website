# **App Name**: Suncity Solar

## Core Features:

- Informational Pages: Create pages for Home, About Us, Products, Services, Projects, Blogs, FAQ, Contact, Terms & Conditions, Performance Guarantee Policy to inform users about Suncity Solar's offerings and policies.
- Product Showcase: Display a range of solar products including panels, inverters, street lights, water heaters, and pumps with detailed specs and benefits.
- Project Gallery: Showcase completed solar projects with images and descriptions to build trust and demonstrate capabilities.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep navy blue (#191970) to reflect professionalism and trustworthiness.
- Background color: Light blue (#E6E6FA), a very desaturated version of the primary.
- Accent color: Bright green (#00FF7F) to highlight eco-friendliness and solar energy benefits.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif for a modern, clean, and readable design.
- Use rounded, modern icons related to solar products, energy efficiency, and sustainability. Product and service icons use colors that clearly distinguish categories while being analogous to the primary color.
- Maintain the same grid layout and spacing as the Solarize theme, with rounded sections and consistent component styling for a cohesive look.
- Incorporate smooth transitions and subtle animations on scrolling and interactions to enhance user experience, consistent with the Solarize theme.
